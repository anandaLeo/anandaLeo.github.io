About

The Leo Club of Ananda College which was initiated few years back became an active club at school during the years of 2012/2013 after a short dormant period. The club which is under the District 306C2 is sponsored by the Lions Club of Nawala. The Leo Club of Ananda College organizes a wide variety of projects annually, mainly under the categories of Child Care, Elder Care, Fundraising and Environmental conservation with the constant guidance of the Teacher In-charge Mrs. Ranjani Mudalige. The Leos of Ananda College have always displayed the true meaning of LEO; Leadership, Experience and Opportunity in anyway and everywhere possible. In order maintain a good reputation; the Leo Club of Ananda College is committed to serve the society with a helping hand. The year 2016/17 will be different for the Leo Club of Ananda College as it is mainly focused under the theme “Making A Difference, Together” and it will be hoping to achieve the goals and objectives despite the all difficulties. The Leo Club of Ananda College is motivated enough to present high values to the society and the nation itself.The Leo Club of Ananda College achieved the following in the past years,

• 2012/2013- Best New Leo Club
• 2013/2014- Best Installation and Best Directory
• 2015/2016- Lions District Convention, Lions District 306C2, Sri lanka Elder Care Project- ‘Wadihiti Sarana’
• Most outstanding alpha leo club-2nd runners up
• ,Most outstanding health and nutrition project-2nd runners up (nirogi diwiyata hetak )
• Higest membetship growth –winner
• Most outstanding serving together project-winner(socialiosis walk)
• Most outstanding helping hand to differently abled & handicapped project-2nd runners up (diwiyata sawiyak)
